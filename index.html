Shadows👻

If you are asked your child knows the pattern or number lock of your smartphone? Then your answer will be 'yes'. This is your yes, there is also a way to go inside the child's smartphone. Children are so smart that even if you change the lock of your phone, they will find out. Many times we ourselves tell the child how to unlock the phone. For this reason, children install games and other apps on their smartphones by themselves. Later they delete it too. Giving smartphones in the hands of children also increases the risk of online fraud or transactions. Many such cases have come to the fore in which the entire bank account of the parents got empty due to one mistake of the child. Children sometimes get addicted to such games, in which children are forced to buy them for the greed of good weapons and to earn points. They do not know that how much money will be spent from the parents' account.
